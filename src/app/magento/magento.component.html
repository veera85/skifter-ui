Magento Training in Chennai

Description

Our Magento training is designed to provide knowledge and skills to become a successful Magento developer. We teach how to develop theme and extensions for Magento and manage the whole site with Admin efficiently. From this Magento course, you can learn to build dynamic shopping websites of any size with our easy modules that cover the installation, customization and other working procedures for creating high-quality magento ecommerce websites. At the end of the training, students will have a knowledge of all the fundamentals and advanced concepts of Magento like Request flow, Rendering systems, Magento database, and Magento Enterprise. Participants will be able to perform simple business logic and work with the database and besides, get an introduction to the Magento API and important system resources and services.
